I am a final-year graduate student in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Princeton University. I received my Bachelor degrees from Tsinghua University (Beijing, China) in Energy and Power Engineering and Economics in July 2017.

My current research spans from chemical kinetics, reactive flows, weakly ionized plasmas to laser diagnostics. I envision immense opportunities for future research in sustainable energy conversion and advanced propulsion.
