I am a fourth-year graduate student in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Princeton University. I received my Bachelor degrees from Tsinghua University (Beijing, China) in Energy and Power Engineering and Economics in July 2017.

My research interests include non-equilibrium plasma and plasma/combustion chemical kinetics.
